Common questions

What does "verified" mean?

Before a listing is published we confirm that the organization exists, that its contact details work, and that the services and age groups listed are accurate. It isn't an endorsement or a quality rating.

A listing has wrong information. What should I do?

Tell us through the feedback form — choose "Correct an existing listing" and name the organization. You can do this anonymously.

I know an organization that should be listed.

Use the feedback form and choose "Suggest an organization to add." You don't need to be connected to it to suggest it.
